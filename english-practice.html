咖啡店 · 餐厅

点餐、修改订单、询问推荐、结账——每次都是真实练习机会

必备短语
Can I get a [drink], please?
我要一杯……（最自然的点餐说法）
What do you recommend?
你有什么推荐的？
I'll have the same.
我要一样的。
Can I make a substitution? / Can I swap [X] for [Y]?
可以换一个食材吗？
Is this dish spicy?
这道菜辣吗？
Could I get the check/bill, please?
麻烦结账（check = 美式；bill = 英式）
Can we split the bill?
可以分开付吗？
I think there might be a mistake with my order.
我觉得我的订单可能有错。
示例对话 · 咖啡店
店员Hi there! What can I get for you today?
你Hi! Can I get a medium latte, please? 点餐我要一杯中杯拿铁，谢谢。
店员Sure! Any milk preference? We have oat, almond, or regular.
你Oat milk, please. And can I get that iced? 追加修改燕麦奶，可以做冰的吗？
店员Of course! Name for the order?
你[Your name]. And can I also grab one of those croissants?顺便买个羊角面包。grab = 拿/要
店员That'll be $7.50.
Cash or card?
你Card, thanks. 结账

购物 · 退换货

问尺码、找东西、退货是被动词汇最多但主动用得最少的场景

必备短语
Do you have this in a [size/color]?
这个有[尺码/颜色]的吗？
I'm just browsing, thanks.
我随便看看，谢谢。（不想被打扰时）
Can I try this on?
我可以试穿吗？
Where are the fitting rooms?
试衣间在哪里？
I'd like to return / exchange this.
我想退货/换货。
I have the receipt.
我有收据。
It doesn't fit / It's the wrong size.
不合身/尺码不对。
Can I get store credit instead?
可以给我换成店内抵用金吗？
示例对话 · 退货
你Hi, I'd like to return this jacket, please. 开门见山
店员Of course! Do you have your receipt?
你Yes, here you go. I bought it last week but it's a bit too small. 说原因，简短即可
店员No problem. Would you like a refund or would you prefer to exchange it for a larger size?
你I'd prefer to exchange it, actually. Do you have it in a large? prefer to = 更倾向于
店员Let me check the back for you.
你Thank you, I appreciate it. 比 "thank you" 更温暖

交通 · 问路

问路和指路——听得出来是另一个难点，要同时处理方向词和指令

必备短语
Excuse me, could you tell me how to get to [place]?
不好意思，请问去[地方]怎么走？
Is it within walking distance?
走路能到吗？
How long does it take?
大概要多久？
Take the [line] toward [direction] and get off at [station].
坐[线路]往[方向]，在[站]下车。
You'll see it on your left/right.
你会看到它在你的左/右边。
Sorry, I'm not from around here.
不好意思，我对这里不熟。（当别人问你时）
Go straight / Turn left / Turn right
直走 / 左转 / 右转
It's right around the corner.
就在转角处，很近。
示例对话 · 问路
你Excuse me! Could you tell me how to get to the Central Library from here? 礼貌开口
路人Sure! Go straight down this street for about two blocks.
你OK — two blocks straight, got it. And then? 复述确认 + 追问
路人Then turn left at the traffic lights. You'll see a coffee shop on the corner — the library is right there on your right. Can't miss it.
你Is it within walking distance, or should I take the subway? 问距离
路人Walking's fine — maybe ten minutes.
你Perfect. Thanks so much!
路人No problem, good luck!

电话 · 客服

没有肢体语言、不能看嘴型、对方语速快——电话是最难的场景之一

必备短语
Hi, I'm calling about [issue].
你好，我打来是关于[问题]的。
I'd like to [make a reservation / cancel / reschedule].
我想[预约/取消/改期]。
Sorry, could you repeat that?
不好意思，能再说一遍吗？
Could you spell that for me?
能帮我拼一下吗？
I'm sorry, I didn't quite catch the last part.
不好意思，最后那部分我没听清。
Let me confirm: [restate what you heard].
让我确认一下：[复述]。
Is there anything else I should know?
还有什么我需要了解的吗？
Thank you for your help. Have a great day!
谢谢你的帮助，祝你今天愉快！
示例对话 · 打电话预约
对方Good afternoon, Dr. Smith's office, how can I help you?
你Hi, I'm calling to make an appointment, please. 开门见山
对方Sure! Is this for a new patient or existing patient?
你Existing. My name is [name]. 直接提供对方需要的信息
对方Great, we have Thursday at 2:15 or Friday at 10:30. Which works better for you?
你Thursday at 2:15 works. 简短确认，不需要解释
对方Perfect! Can I get a callback number?
你Sure, it's [number]. Could you read that back to me? 主动要求确认，专业做法

看医生 · 药店

描述症状、理解诊断、问用药——关键时刻不能靠手势

必备短语
I have a [headache / sore throat / fever / stomachache].
我[头疼/喉咙痛/发烧/胃疼]。
It started [X] days ago.
从[X]天前开始的。
It gets worse when I [do something].
当我[做某事]时会加重。
I'm allergic to [penicillin / shellfish / nuts].
我对[青霉素/贝类/坚果]过敏。
I'm on medication for [condition].
我在为[病症]服药。
What are the side effects?
有什么副作用？
How many times a day should I take this?
每天要吃几次？
Do I need a prescription for this?
这个需要处方吗？
示例对话 · 看诊
医生So, what brings you in today?
你I have a sore throat and a low fever. It started about three days ago. 描述症状 + 起始时间我喉咙痛，有点低烧，大概三天前开始的。
医生Are you having any other symptoms? Cough, runny nose?
你A little bit of a cough, but no runny nose. The throat pain gets worse when I swallow. 加重情况
医生Any allergies to medications?
你I'm allergic to penicillin. 过敏信息要主动说
医生Got it. I'll prescribe a different antibiotic. Take it twice a day for five days.
你Should I take it with food? And what are the side effects? 问用药方式和副作用
医生Yes, take it with food to avoid an upset stomach. You might feel a little drowsy.
你OK, understood. Thank you, doctor.

打招呼 · 初次见面

第一次自我介绍——大多数人准备的是内容，但忘了准备接话和提问

自我介绍框架
Hi, I'm [name]. Nice to meet you!
基础款，但后面一定要加问题
I work in / I'm studying [field].
说职业/专业，不要只说公司名字
I'm based in [city], but I'm originally from [place].
说两个地方更有话题
What about you? / And you are...?
自我介绍后立刻反问，是社交礼仪
Oh, interesting! How long have you been doing that?
接话的万能句，表现真实兴趣
How do you know [host/organizer]?
活动/派对上认识新人的开场问题
示例对话 · 活动上初次认识
AHi! I don't think we've met — I'm Jamie. 主动开口，不等对方
BOh, hi! I'm Sam. Nice to meet you.
AAre you here for the design conference too? 用场景建立共同话题
BYeah! First time here, actually. What about you?
ASecond year. It gets better, I promise! 分享信息 + 幽默，建立好感 So what do you do in design?
BI'm a UX designer at a startup. You?
AOh nice, I'm in product. We probably have a lot to talk about! 建立连接点

Small Talk · 日常闲聊

不是浪费时间——Small talk 是建立关系的基础设施，缺少它会让人觉得你冷漠

Small Talk 的结构：开话题 → 给一点自己的信息 → 问对方一个问题 → 接话并延续。缺少任何一步，对话都会死掉。最常见的问题是只回答不反问，让对方觉得你不感兴趣。
按场景分组的 Small Talk 句型
How's your week going?
你这周怎么样？（周中万能问法）
Not too bad — pretty busy, but good. You?
还好，有点忙，但不错。你呢？（回答模板：总体 + 细节 + 反问）
How was your weekend? / Any fun plans this weekend?
周末过得怎么样？/ 周末有什么计划？
I heard you went to [place] — how was it?
我听说你去了[地方]——怎么样？
Did you catch the game / that show last night?
你昨晚看球了吗/看那个节目了吗？
Have you tried that new [restaurant/place] yet?
你去了那家新开的[餐厅/地方]了吗？
I know what you mean. / Tell me about it.
我懂你。/ 可不是吗。（表达共鸣）
Oh nice, how did that go?
哦不错，然后呢 / 那怎么样了？（延续话题的万能接话）
示例对话 · 周一早上同事闲聊
AMorning! How was your weekend?
BPretty good! We went hiking on Saturday — first time in months. 总体 + 细节 It was tiring but so worth it. You?
AOh nice! Where'd you go? Where'd = Where did（口语缩读）
BJust the hills outside the city. Nothing fancy, but the view was amazing. not fancy, but... = 自谦后给亮点
AI've been meaning to do that. Maybe I'll finally drag myself out there next weekend. I've been meaning to = 我一直想做但还没做
BYou should! It's worth it. Anyway, ready for the week? Anyway = 自然转换话题
AHa, barely. Coffee first. 幽默收尾，显得轻松

夸人 · 接受赞美

英语文化里夸人比中文更频繁、更直接——接受赞美也有固定套路

夸人的表达
That's a great [idea / point / question]!
这个[想法/观点/问题]很棒！
You did a really good job on [X].
你[X]做得真的很好。
I love your [outfit / jacket / style].
我很喜欢你的[穿搭/夹克/风格]。
That presentation was really impressive.
那次演示真的很出色。
接受赞美（不要只说 "thank you"，要加一句）
Thanks! I really appreciate that.
谢谢！这对我来说很重要。
Oh, thanks! I actually got it from [place].
谢谢！这是从[地方]买的。（衣服被夸时）
That means a lot coming from you.
你这样说让我很开心。（对很尊重的人）
Aw, you're too kind!
哎呀，你太客气了！（友好场合）
⚠ 中国人常说"哪里哪里"，但英语里否认赞美会让对方尴尬
文化差异：中文习惯谦虚否认赞美（"哪里哪里，我还差得远"），但英语文化里直接谢谢并接受才是礼貌。拒绝赞美会让对方觉得他的判断力被否定。
示例对话 · 夸人与接受赞美
AHey, your presentation this morning was really impressive. The data visualizations were so clear. 具体夸到细节，比 "good job" 有力多了
BOh, thank you! That means a lot — I spent a lot of time on those charts. 接受 + 分享一个背后的故事，不否认
AIt showed! And I love your jacket, by the way. The color is great on you.
BAw, thanks! I actually just got it last weekend — I wasn't sure about the color at first. 接受 + 加一句细节，对话自然延续
AWell, it was definitely the right call!
常见错误：被夸时说"No no, it wasn't that good"或"I still have a lot to improve"——这在英语文化里会让对方觉得尴尬，好像他们的称赞是错的。直接说谢谢，加一句话，就够了。

人多嘈杂、互相不认识——需要主动开话题的能力，以及如何自然离开对话

派对和社交场合常用句
How do you know [host's name]?
你怎么认识[主人]的？（派对上最好用的破冰问题）
Have you been to one of these before?
你之前来过这种[活动]吗？
What do you do when you're not [at this event]?
你平时做什么？（比"你是做什么的"更轻松）
I'm going to grab another drink — it was really nice meeting you!
我去拿杯饮料——认识你真的很高兴！（礼貌离开对话）
I should go catch up with [person] — let's exchange numbers!
我得去找[某人]聊聊——我们留个联系方式吧！
This is [friend's name] — you two should definitely talk!
这是[朋友名字]——你们两个绝对应该聊聊！（介绍朋友认识）

你I'm good, thanks! But let's keep talking — I want to hear about the startup. 礼貌留住对话

职场 Networking

Networking 最难的不是自我介绍，而是找到共同话题并保持对话

关键短语
What brings you to [event]?
你是为什么来这个活动的？
What kind of work do you do?
你是做什么工作的？（比"What's your job"更自然）
That's interesting — how did you get into that?
那很有意思——你是怎么进入这个行业的？
I'd love to hear more about that sometime.
我很想之后多了解一下。（留下联系的铺垫）
Can I get your LinkedIn / card?
可以加你 LinkedIn / 要你名片吗？
I'll reach out — great meeting you!
我会联系你的——很高兴认识你！

你Mostly to meet people in the AI space — we're starting to build out some ML features. What about you? 说目的 + 反问，对话不停
BSame, honestly. We're exploring how to integrate AI into our product roadmap.
你That's interesting — how did you get into product after a data background? 问职业路径，话题自然深入
BLong story — happy to tell you. I'd love to hear more about your ML work too.
你Definitely. Can I get your LinkedIn? I think there's a lot we could learn from each other. 自然收尾 + 留联系
BAbsolutely, here you go. I'll reach out this week.

给反馈 · 接受批评

直接批评和委婉批评之间有很多层——英语职场文化更倾向于建设性框架

给建设性反馈
I think this is really solid. One thing I'd consider changing is...
我觉得这个很扎实。有一点我会考虑改一下……
This is great — could we also think about...?
这个很好——我们是否也可以考虑一下……？
The structure works well. My only concern is [specific issue].
结构很好。我唯一的顾虑是[具体问题]。
What if we tried [alternative approach]?
如果我们试试[另一个方案]呢？
接受批评
Thanks for the feedback — that's a fair point.
感谢反馈——这一点说得有道理。
I hadn't thought of it that way.
我之前没有从这个角度想。
Let me think about how to address that.
让我想想怎么处理这个问题。
I'll take that on board.
我会把这一点纳入考虑。（正式但常用）
示例对话 · 给与接受反馈
AHey, do you have five minutes? I wanted to share some thoughts on the report. 提前预约，不突然
BSure, go ahead.
AOverall I think the analysis is really solid — the insights on the Q3 data are great. 先肯定，找真实亮点 One thing I'd consider changing is the structure of the second section. It felt a little hard to follow.
BOK, can you say more about what felt unclear? 接受 + 追问细节，不立刻防御
ASure — I think leading with the conclusion and then the supporting data might work better. Right now it builds up slowly and the key point comes at the end.
BThat's a fair point — I hadn't thought about it from a reader's perspective. 承认对方说得有道理，不抗拒 I'll restructure it that way.
AGreat. The content itself is strong — this will just make it land better.
给反馈的结构：先说一个真实的亮点 → 用 "one thing I'd consider" 或 "my only concern is" 引出问题 → 给具体建议，不只是批评。这个结构在英语职场里通用，不会让对方觉得被攻击。
